SCHOLARS

Scholars

(3 – 6 year old Kindergarten-aged children)

  • The program is tailored for kindergarten-aged learners, to provide a solid foundation for English listening, speaking, reading and writing.
  • Trainers encourage learners to start communicating in English and foster a life-long enthusiasm for the language.
  • The Scholars program is divided into three levels. Each level comprises 96 classroom hours and takes approximately 1 year to complete.

 

Scholars 1

The start of a learner’s English education, which includes phonics, writing, authentic language, and early reading and conversation

Scholars 2

Strengthen the learner’s English through the teaching of complete sentence structures, reading comprehension, punctuation, sight word recognition, and grammar rules

Scholars 3

Increased emphasis on accurate grammar, conversation, spelling, writing descriptive sentences and following verbal and written instructions in English
